## 10. What to do if we need to use the ticket again for the access?

Kindly remind the team to uncheck the “CLOSE TICKET” box. Afterwards, go to Menu > Ticket list, and click “REOPEN” so that they can reuse the ticket until its validity date. Maximum validity of access ticket is 10 days. Request a new ticket access if the activity would go beyond the 10 days.

For new registration:
-      Company ID
-      Working at Height certificate (WAH)
-      Certificate of Employment (COE)
-      NBI Clearance
For access request:
-      MOP/SOW (Detailed information of activity)
-      RAAWA (Globe)

## 12. Why can’t we request access for installation and dismantling activities?

You need to provide the approved change request application number.

## 13. Why can't we request access for pullout activities?

Fill out the FTAP Extraction Form, complete all details and have it signed to your authorized signatories. Email the form to Site Access Team and they will route it for approval internally. Once approved, you may now file for work order/ticket access.
